Some Former WWE Champions

From most recent to least recent

Cody Rhodes

Cody Rhodes is the current reigning Universal Champion of the WWE. He won his belt on April 7, 2024 at Wrestlemania XL.

Roman Reigns

Roman Reigns was the Undisputed WWE Universal CHampion from April 3rd, 2022 to April 7th, 2024. He won this title at Wrestlemania 38.

Brock Lesnar

Brock Lesnar was the WWE Champion a number of times, most recently being from February 19, 2022 to April 3rd, 2022. He has also won titles in the UFC.
