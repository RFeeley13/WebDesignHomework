How to win Street Fighter 6 at Evolution 2024

Step-by-step Guide

What is needed

  • Controller to play the game
    • Leverless
    • Pad
    • Stick
  • Enough sanity to pick Luke on the character select

First: Select Luke against any other character and go to training mode

None of the other training settings matter, including what stage you picked.

  • However, make sure you set the punishment settings to "Punish Counter" for best results

Second: Press Crouching Medium Punch

This normal punch button is broken because:

  • It has a far reach
  • Is hit confirmable into Special moves and Drive Rush cancels
  • It has a 6 frame start up (very fast)
  • It is hard for the opponent to whiff punish if you miss

Third: Combo into Super

At this point you can just mash an easy combo out that will do damage equivalent to any other character performing a combo as difficult as open heart surgery

Fourth: Contemplate your sins

The amount of mental damage you will have done to your opponent at this point will leave them wanting to quit the game

Final Step

Navigate your way to Evo 2024, happening July 19-21 in Las Vegas, Nevada. From here all you have to do is land crouch medium punch as detailed above to win insane prize money and a legacy worth remembering.
